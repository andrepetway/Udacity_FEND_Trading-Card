Red Panda

Don't let its name fool you, the red panda has a closer affinity to a raccoon, weasel, or skunk. It is not related to a panda.

  • Scientific Name: Ailurus Fulgens
  • Maximum Length: 63.5cm/25" (Body), 48.5cm/19.1" (Tail)
  • Described By: Frederic Cuvier
  • Natural Habitat: Eastern Himalayas and southwestern China

A small mammal native to east Asia, the red panda has dense reddish-brown fur with a black belly and legs, white-lined ears, a mostly white muzzle, a ringed tail, flexible joints, and semi-retractable claws.
